About Britney Spears

Britney Spears is an American singer, dancer, and pop icon born on December 2, 1981, in McComb, Mississippi, and was raised in Kentwood, Louisiana. She rose to fame in the late 1990s with her debut album, "Baby One More Time," which became a global sensation. Known for her catchy songs and electrifying performances, Britney dominated the pop scene with hits like "Toxic," "Oops!... I Did It Again," and "Womanizer." She remains one of the most celebrated and influential pop stars in history.
